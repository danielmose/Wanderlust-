Our Mission

MindBridge was born from a simple belief: quality mental health support should be accessible to every Kenyan, regardless of income, location, or stigma.

🌿

Why MindBridge?

Kenya faces a severe mental health crisis — fewer than 100 psychiatrists serve a population of over 55 million. MindBridge bridges this gap using compassionate AI triage, community peer support, and scalable digital tools.

We're not replacing therapists. We're making sure people don't fall through the cracks while waiting for one. Our AI screens, supports, educates, and connects — 24 hours a day.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MindBridge and how it works.

Is MindBridge a replacement for therapy?+

No. MindBridge is a triage and support tool, not a clinical service. We help you understand your needs, access coping resources, and connect to professionals. For serious mental health conditions, we will always recommend — and help you access — licensed therapists and psychiatrists.

Is my information private and confidential?+

Yes, completely. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell your data to third parties. The only time we would share information is if we believed there was an immediate risk to your life or someone else's — and we would tell you that clearly in our terms.

How does the AI chatbot work?+

Our chatbot is powered by Google Gemini AI, prompted with mental health support guidelines, Kenyan context, and crisis-detection protocols. It provides empathetic, evidence-informed responses. It also detects signs of crisis and surfaces emergency resources immediately.

Can I use MindBridge in Swahili?+

Yes! The AI chatbot understands and can respond in both English and Swahili. Simply type in whichever language feels most comfortable — the AI will match you.

What happens in a mental health emergency?+

If the AI detects a crisis — such as suicidal ideation or self-harm — it immediately provides Kenya-specific emergency contacts including MindBridge Hotline (0768168973) and directs you to the nearest emergency service. Your safety is always the top priority.

How do I upgrade or cancel my plan?+

You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your dashboard settings. There are no cancellation fees. If you cancel a paid plan, you retain access until the end of your billing period.

Is MindBridge available across all Kenyan counties?+

Yes — because MindBridge is digital, it's available anywhere with an internet or data connection. We also offer a lightweight WhatsApp mode for users with limited data plans, which uses minimal bandwidth.
